VISUAL STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- Clean, modern flat design with a site-plan / architectural map aesthetic.
- The overall impression is a TOP-DOWN CAMPUS MAP showing where ICT equipment is physically located across the complex.
- The central hub should be visually prominent — the most important element.
- Multiple building nodes arranged spatially to convey "complex" rather than a single building.
- Equipment icons must be actual recognizable illustrations: camera on pole, parcel locker box, parking gate arm, antenna tower, card reader unit, UPS battery bank — NOT generic rectangles.
- All text in Korean (한국어). Clean sans-serif font.
- Subtle faint site plan in background gives spatial context.
- Dashed lines for underground cable routes give engineering authenticity.
- Color palette: Dark Green (#0A2E1A), Gold (#C8960C), Teal (#008080), Blue (#0066CC), Orange (#FF6600), Purple (#7700CC), Warm White (#F9F9F6).
- Image ratio: wide landscape (16:9).
- Professional engineering poster quality, suitable for a building management manual.
